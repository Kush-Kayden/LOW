Frequently Asked Questions

Solar energy is not only a renewable resource but using solar can also help you save a ton of money on your energy bills. Solar energy is a free source of energy that can be used to power your home or business — just pay for the initial equipment, installation, and any maintenance down the road.

The average lifespan of a solar panel is about 25-30 years, so once you make the initial investment, you can enjoy decades of free energy.

Solar panels don’t require a lot of maintenance. You should perform a visual inspection of your solar panels at least once a year to check for any damage or debris. If you live in an area with heavy snowfall, you may need to brush the snow off your panels in the winter. Other than that, solar panel maintenance is typically minimal.
